Educational Divisions

We structure our divisions to align with student cognitive milestones, encouraging independence and exploration.

Rigorous Curriculum Design

Horizon Academy employs an integrated international curriculum accredited by global councils. Our academic calendar combines Core Subjects, Advanced Electives, and Interactive Projects designed to build research capability and team leadership.

Primary (Grades 1-5)

Focuses on primary language arts, math numeracy, spatial reasoning, and social foundations.

  • Guided Inquiry Methods
  • Creative Arts Integration
  • Foundational Literacy
  • Emotional Growth Care

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Transitions students toward independent research, laboratory science, and structured collaboration.

  • Hands-on Science Labs
  • Pre-Algebra & Geometry
  • Introductory Coding
  • Public Speaking Seminars

High School (Grades 9-12)

Focuses on college readiness, Advanced Placement (AP) electives, and leadership project execution.

  • Advanced AP Pathways
  • College Advising Program
  • Thesis Research Project
  • Community Leadership
