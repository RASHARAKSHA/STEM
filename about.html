Our Legacy

Our Journey of Excellence

Horizon Academy was founded in 2005 with a clear, ambitious mission: to offer a comprehensive, child-centered educational experience that challenges minds and fosters noble character. From our modest beginnings with 50 students, we have expanded to serve a vibrant community of over 1,200 learners.

Our curriculum blends traditional academic rigor with modern, inquiry-based STEM and liberal arts programs. Located on a beautifully landscaped 12-acre campus, we provide students with state-of-the-art labs, creative arts studios, and athletic centers designed to support growth inside and outside the classroom.

World-Class Facilities

We believe learning environments shape student outcomes. Our modern facilities inspire academic focus and creative expression.

The Rose Library

A multi-story, quiet study sanctuary containing 30,000+ volumes, private study pods, and collaborative workspace media tables.

Science & Innovation Wing

State-of-the-art laboratories equipped for hands-on college-level chemistry, biology, physics projects, and robotics builds.

Performing Arts Auditorium

A beautiful 500-seat auditorium with professional acoustic panels, digital sound boards, and full backstage theater suites.
